WHO ARE WE?

Pepsi, one of the world's most renowned beverage brands, has a rich and fascinating history. It all began in 1893 when Caleb Bradham, a pharmacist from North Carolina, introduced "Brad's Drink" as a digestive aid. Later renamed Pepsi-Cola in 1898, the soda gained popularity for its refreshing taste and carbonation. Over the years, Pepsi underwent various ownership changes and marketing strategies, but it wasn't until the 1950s that it emerged as a major competitor to its rival, Coca-Cola. Pepsi's iconic "Pepsi Generation" campaign in the 1960s captured the spirit of youthful exuberance and resonated with the changing cultural landscape. The brand continued to innovate and expand its product offerings, introducing Diet Pepsi in the 1960s and Pepsi Max in 1993. Today, Pepsi is a global beverage leader, offering a wide range of carbonated and non-carbonated drinks, and its distinctive logo is recognized worldwide, symbolizing refreshment, energy, and a zest for life..


PRODUCTS



PEPSI COLA

Pepsi Cola is a popular carbonated soft drink known for its sweet and refreshing taste. It was introduced in 1893 and is now widely consumed globally.


PEPSI MAX

Pepsi Max is a sugar-free and calorie-free variant of the popular carbonated soft drink, Pepsi Cola. It offers the same great taste as regular Pepsi but without the added sugar. Pepsi Max is designed for those who want to enjoy the refreshing flavor of Pepsi while watching their calorie intake.


CHERRY PEPSI

Cherry Pepsi is a delightful variation of the classic carbonated soft drink, Pepsi Cola. It combines the familiar taste of Pepsi with a burst of juicy cherry flavor. The result is a refreshing and slightly sweet beverage that adds a fruity twist to the original Pepsi experience. Cherry Pepsi is a popular choice for those seeking a delicious and vibrant alternative to traditional cola drinks.


MANGO PEPSI

Mango Pepsi is a flavorful variant of the popular carbonated soft drink, Pepsi Cola. It offers a unique twist by infusing the refreshing taste of mango into the classic Pepsi formula. Mango Pepsi provides a tropical and fruity experience for those seeking a different flavor profile.
